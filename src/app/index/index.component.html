WELCOME TO THE OOTP CALCULATOR!

POSITION CALCULATOR

I created this site because I always found myself wondering how good my OOTP players could be if I changed their position. Thanks to a little experimentation, I was able to figure out the formulas to calculate players' maximum possible position ratings based on their current defensive ratings.

Right now, you can use the position calculator to determine exactly how good your players could be at any position.


PROJECTIONS (UPDATE 12/28)
